# WhatsApp OB Group Analysis Summary

Based on the analysis of WhatsApp groups with "(OB)" in their name over the past 5 days, using a 30-minute SLA during working hours (10AM-7PM IST), we found:

## Key Findings

1. **Total Groups Analyzed:** 271 WhatsApp groups
2. **Classification Results:**
   - **Needs Attention:** 65 merchants (24.0%)
   - **At Risk:** 0 merchants (0.0%)
   - **Stable Merchants:** 206 merchants (76.0%)

## SLA Performance

- No merchants were found to be "At Risk" solely based on response time violations of the 30-minute SLA during 10AM-7PM IST working hours.
- This suggests that Shopflo support teams are generally responsive during business hours.
- Merchants with negative sentiment were classified as "Needs Attention" regardless of response times.

## Common Issues Detected

Based on sentiment analysis, the most frequent issues mentioned in conversations include:

1. Technical problems with checkout functionality
2. Integration issues with Shopify and other platforms
3. Urgent requests for assistance with critical business operations
4. Concerns about payment processing and order management
5. Questions about configuration and features

## Recommendations

1. **Response Time Optimization:**
   - Continue maintaining the strong response rate within the 30-minute SLA
   - Implement pre-emptive monitoring for issues that might require attention

2. **Proactive Support:**
   - Reach out to the 65 merchants in the "Needs Attention" category with personalized support
   - Address the underlying issues causing negative sentiment

3. **Process Improvements:**
   - Document and analyze the technical issues to identify patterns
   - Develop a knowledge base of common solutions to reduce resolution time

## Next Steps

1. Implement continuous monitoring with the 30-minute SLA threshold
2. Assign dedicated support staff to resolve issues for merchants needing attention
3. Develop automated alerts when customer sentiment turns negative
4. Schedule weekly reviews of SLA performance and merchant satisfaction
